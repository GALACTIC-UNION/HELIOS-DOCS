1. **Undercollateralization** (UST/LUNA — algorithmic, collapsed 2022)
2. **Centralization** (USDT/USDC — single-entity reserve custody)
3. **Static policy** (DAI — rule-based, cannot adapt to macroeconomic shocks)

HELIOS CODEX solves all three:
1. **200% over-collateralization** by diversified RWA basket
2. **Federated policy** across 8 central bank nodes
3. **ATLAS AGI** — dynamic monetary policy that adapts in real-time
